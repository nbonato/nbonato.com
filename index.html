European Schoolnet

I currently work at European Schoolnet on the European School Education Platform, the home of eTwinning. eTwinning is an online space for schools' collaboration.

I help define technical requirements and review user stories. I also assist in planning user experience improvements with more senior team members.

I support the platform's analytics using Piwik and PowerBI. Among other things, I help the communication and outreach teams understand platform usage.

I also act as an internal consultant for HTML and web content, supporting different teams when needed.

As a web editor, I convert storyboards from pedagogical teams into Moodle courses, using custom-built components.

I started building a reusable internal library of these components using MkDocs to speed up the process for me and other web editors.
